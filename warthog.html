WARTHOG

The common warthog is a wild member of the pig family found in grassland, savanna, and woodland in sub-Saharan Africa. In the past, it was commonly treated as a subspecies of P. aethiopicus, but today that scientific name is restricted to the desert warthog of northern Kenya, Somalia, and eastern Ethiopia. Trophic level: Herbivorous Encyclopedia of Life Species: P. africanus Class: Mammalia Order: Artiodactyla Phylum: Chordata Family: Suidae Mass: 60 – 150 kg (Adult), 45 – 75 kg (Adult)
