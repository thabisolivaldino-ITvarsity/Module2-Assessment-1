ANIMALS MAP PLACES

The tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Panthera. It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange fur with a white underside. It is an apex predator, primarily preying on ungulates such as deer and wild boar. Species: P. tigris Conservation status: Endangered (Population decreasing) Encyclopedia of Life Lifespan: 8 – 10 years (In the wild) Speed: 49 – 65 km/h (Adult, In Short Bursts) Mass: 90 – 310 kg (Adult), 65 – 180 kg (Adult) Length: 2,5 – 3,9 m (Adult), 2 – 2,8 m (Adult)
